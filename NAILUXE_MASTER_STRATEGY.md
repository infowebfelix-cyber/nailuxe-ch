## 1. STRATEGIC FOUNDATION

### 1.1 Market Thesis

Switzerland's nail care market is fragmented, dominated by low-to-mid tier studios with no dominant premium player. The affluent Swiss female consumer — the most demanding, highest-spending, and most brand-loyal segment in personal care — has no studio worthy of her standards.

Nailuxe closes this gap.

The opportunity is not to compete on price or volume. It is to own the premium positioning entirely, before any competitor identifies and occupies it. First-mover advantage in the Swiss luxury nail care segment is Nailuxe's strategic window.

## 6. TECHNOLOGY DECISION: HOSTINGER WEBSITE BUILDER vs. NODE.JS WEB APP

This is a foundational decision. The right choice depends on your current phase, development resources, and 24-month digital roadmap.

---

### 6.1 RECOMMENDATION: **Node.js Web App on Hostinger**

**Verdict:** Build a custom Node.js application. Not the website builder.

**Reasoning:**

| Factor                          | Website Builder               | Node.js Web App                              |
|---------------------------------|-------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Brand control                   | Template-constrained          | Complete pixel-perfect control               |
| Performance                     | Moderate (shared infra)       | Optimized — critical for luxury brand feel   |
| CRM integration                 | Limited / no custom logic     | Full custom integration capability           |
| Booking system                  | Third-party widget embed only | Native or deeply integrated                  |
| Analytics embedding             | Surface-level                 | Full data pipeline control                   |
| Multilingual (DE/FR/EN)         | Basic, often broken UX        | Proper i18n framework (next-intl, etc.)      |
| SEO control                     | Limited                       | Full technical SEO ownership                 |
| Scalability to Phase 2+         | Requires rebuild               | Extends cleanly                              |
| Luxury brand credibility        | Undermined by builder tells   | Indistinguishable from world-class brands    |
| Long-term cost                  | Monthly builder fee forever   | One-time build + hosting only                |

**The builder is appropriate for a test or MVP. Nailuxe is not a test. The website IS the brand at launch.**

---

### 6.2 RECOMMENDED TECH STACK

**Framework:** Next.js 14+ (React, App Router)
- Server-side rendering for SEO and performance
- Built-in image optimization (critical for photography-first brand)
- API routes for backend logic (booking webhooks, CRM sync)
- Excellent i18n support via `next-intl`

**Hosting:** Hostinger VPS (KVM 2 or KVM 4 plan)
- Full Node.js support
- SSH access, custom domains, SSL
- Sufficient for Phase 1–2 traffic
- Cost-effective for Swiss startup stage

**Database:** PostgreSQL (via Hostinger or Supabase)
- Client profiles, booking records, loyalty data
- Structured, queryable — essential for your analytics work

**CMS (Content):** Sanity.io or Contentful
- Brand-controlled content updates without code deployments
- Multilingual content model (DE/FR/EN)
- Image pipeline with CDN delivery

**Booking System Integration:** Acuity Scheduling or Fresha
- Acuity: Premium positioning, highly customizable, API available
- Fresha: Industry-specific, free at base tier, strong nail/beauty features
- **Recommendation:** Acuity for Phase 1 (brand-aligned UX), evaluate Fresha at Phase 2 for multi-location management

**CRM:** HubSpot (Starter) or a custom Supabase-backed solution
- HubSpot Starter: Fast to deploy, solid for Phase 1, good email automation
- Custom: Higher upfront investment, complete data ownership, aligns with your analytics work
- **Recommendation:** HubSpot Starter for Phase 1 → migrate to custom analytics-integrated CRM at Phase 2

**Email:** Resend or Postmark (transactional) + Brevo (marketing campaigns)

**Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 + Mixpanel (event tracking) + custom dashboard (Metabase or Superset over your PostgreSQL)

**Payments:** Stripe
- Handles CHF natively
- Supports subscriptions (loyalty membership billing)
- Strong European compliance (PCI DSS, SCA/3DS2)

## 10. NAILUXE STUDIO STANDARD (NSS)

The NSS is the non-negotiable operational blueprint for every Nailuxe location. It covers 7 domains:

### NSS Domain 1: Physical Environment
- Interior design specification: materials, lighting temperature (2700–3000K warm white), scent (signature diffuser blend), sound (curated ambient playlist, max 60dB), seating specification
- Minimum cleanliness standard: surfaces cleaned between every client, tools sterilized to Swiss SIA/OFSP standards
- Product display: visible, curated, never cluttered

### NSS Domain 2: Service Delivery
- Every client greeted within 15 seconds of entry
- Client name used a minimum of 3 times per session (first greeting, mid-service, farewell)
- Beverage offered within 2 minutes of seating (still water, sparkling, herbal tea)
- No phone use by artists during active service
- No rushed service completion — session times are minimum guaranteed, not rushed targets

### NSS Domain 3: Product Standard
- Product list approved by CEO — deviations require written justification
- Only products with EU/Swiss cosmetic compliance certification in use
- No substitutions without client notification if preferred product is unavailable

### NSS Domain 4: Staff Conduct
- Dress code: All black, clean, minimal accessories
- Language: Formal register with new clients, adapt to client's preference over time
- No personal calls during shift
- Conflict resolution: De-escalate, never argue, escalate to Studio Director if needed

### NSS Domain 5: Booking & CRM
- All client data entered into CRM within 24h of first visit
- Post-visit follow-up: sent within 48h, personalized to service received
- No double-bookings — system locks prevent it
- Cancellation policy: Communicated at booking, enforced consistently

### NSS Domain 6: Hygiene Protocol
- Full station reset between clients: 8-minute minimum
- Implements: Single-use or autoclave-sterilized per session
- Nail dust extraction: Active ventilation per station
- Hand hygiene: Client hand wash before service, artist minimum 20-second hand wash
